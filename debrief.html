Speed Networking Debrief

January 22, 2018 at HubSpot

Venue

  • 3 rows of tables → each row sat 28 people
  • 4 chairs per table, facing eachother → in total we could fit 84 attendees

Each table was labeled green, blue, or pink and had pens and paper on it so people could exchange info. We also printed the speed networking questions from the CO guide.

Seat Assignments

Nametags had either a green, blue, or pink sticker:

... to denote the green, blue, or pink table row

Networking Rounds

  • Each round was 4 minutes long (check out the countdown timer)
  • People facing the front of the room moved after each 4 minute round, while people facing the back of the room stayed still
  • Had a "backup" volunteer to speed network if there were an odd number of attendees
